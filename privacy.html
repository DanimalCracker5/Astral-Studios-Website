PRIVACY POLICY

How Astral Studios handles your data.

Overview

Astral Studios is a one-person independent studio operated by Daniel Gentry. This privacy policy explains what data is collected when you use the Astral game or this website, and how it is used.

If you have questions or concerns, contact us directly.

Data We Collect

The Astral game uses Microsoft Azure PlayFab for backend services including player accounts, leaderboards, and game state. PlayFab may collect a player ID, device information, and gameplay data necessary to operate the game. This data is not sold or shared with third parties.

On Android, the game uses IronSource (LevelPlay) to serve ads. IronSource may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

This website does not use cookies or tracking scripts beyond what is required to serve the page.

Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are used and have their own privacy policies: Microsoft Azure PlayFab (game backend), IronSource / LevelPlay (ads on Android), Dissonance (voice chat), and Netlify (website hosting).

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your game data at any time. Visit the Delete My Data page or contact us directly with your request.

Children

Astral is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and it will be removed promptly.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, use the contact page.
