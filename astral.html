About

Astral is an open world sandbox game with an astral projection ability — the ability to leave your body and explore the world.

Developed by Daniel Gentry, with the help of tools like Universal Vehicle Controller by Perfect Games, VR Interaction Framework, and Art assets by synty studios. The game will continue to grow and improve — news is posted here.

Currently in early development. Bugs and unfinished features should be expected. Mini-games and jobs are a work in progree.

Game Modes
🌍
Sandbox
Open-world free play with an in-game economy and currency system.
🧱
Free Build
Unlimited access to all placeable items with no restrictions.
🚗
Drive
Open world vehicle driving. Purchase vehicles from the in-game shop with earned currency.
⚔️
Arenas
Wave-based enemy combat. Survive as long as you can — when you die, the run ends.
