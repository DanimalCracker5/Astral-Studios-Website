How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data, use the contact page and include the following in your message: the platform you played on (Android, Steam, Meta Quest), your in-game username or player ID if you have it, and a description of what you'd like deleted.

Requests are handled personally and you'll receive a confirmation once the data has been removed. Response time is typically within a few business days.

What Gets Deleted

Upon request, the following can be deleted: your PlayFab player account and associated game data (progress, currency, settings), any information submitted through this website's contact or bug report forms.

Note that data held by third-party services (such as Google Play, Steam, or Meta) is managed by those platforms directly and must be deleted through their respective account settings.

Questions

For anything else related to your data, see the Privacy Policy or get in touch.
